I am a Professor of Mathematical Engineering at the National Autonomous University of Honduras, and
I conduct research in matrix analysis and its applications in scientific and technical computing.

My main research areas are: structured matrix approximation, model order reduction of dynamical
systems, and matrix methods in predictive numerical simulation.
