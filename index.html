I am a Professor of Mathematical Engineering at the National Autonomous University of Honduras and I conduct research in matrix analysis, and its applications in scientific and technical computing. My main research areas are: Structured matrix approximation, Model order reduction of discrete-time control systems, and Topological methods in structural and continuum mechanics.
