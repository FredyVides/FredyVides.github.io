I am an Operator Theorist and I serve as Professor of Mathematical Engineering and Researcher at the
National Autonomous University of Honduras. I conduct research in operator approximation theory
and its applications in system identification and scientific machine learning.

My main research areas are: structured operator approximation, process optimization, numerical data
analysis, system identification, model reduction and system approximation. I am particularly interested
in scientific machine learning and sparse model identification.

I am currently serving as coordinator of the Scientific Computing Innovation Center at UNAH Tegugicalpa.
I am also a member of the international scientific committee of the journal Revista de Matemática:
Teoría y Aplicaciones of the University of Costa Rica.
