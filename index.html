I am a Professor of Mathematical Engineering at the National Autonomous University of Honduras, and
I conduct research in matrix analysis, numerical optimization, systems theory and their applications
in data analysis, scientific and technical computing.

My main research areas are: structured matrix approximation, process optimization, numerical data
analysis, system identification, model reduction and system approximation.
