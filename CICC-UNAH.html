I am a Professor of Mathematical Engineering at the National Autonomous University of Honduras, and I conduct research in matrix analysis and its applications in scientific and technical computing. My main research areas are: Structured matrix approximation, Model order reduction of dynamical systems, and Matrix methods in predictive numerical simulation.
